AQ: Jaern Helper Tools

This website's purpose is to provide tools to make running and playing AQ: Jaern a little easier. On it, you'll find:

  • Character Generator: generate stat lines for one or multiple characters.
  • Multiattack Roller: a roller for rolling multiple attacks at once.
  • Contested Checker: a roller that will roll a contested check for you, with the option of having it roll for both you and your opponent.
  • Gradual Checker: a roller that will roll a gradual check for you.
  • Critical Roller: a roller for getting a critical hit result from one of the tables.
